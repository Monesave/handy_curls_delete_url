About HandyCurls

HandyCurls is a mobile application that connects service providers with customers, allowing users to book and manage various services with ease.

Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your account and personal data from HandyCurls by submitting the form below. Once we receive your request, we will process it according to our privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

Please Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This will remove all your personal information, profile details, transaction history, and other data associated with your account.

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile will be removed from our platform
  • Your personal information will be deleted from our systems
  • Your transaction history will be anonymized
  • You will no longer have access to the HandyCurls service

Deletion Request Form

After submitting your request, you will receive a confirmation email. We will process your deletion request within 14 business days and send you a final confirmation once completed.

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at support@monesave.com.
